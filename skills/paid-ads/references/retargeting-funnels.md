# Retargeting Funnels & Performance Benchmarks

Three-stage retargeting funnel structure with budget allocation and industry ROAS benchmarks. Use for campaign architecture recommendations.

---

## Three-Stage Retargeting Funnel

### Stage 1: Awareness (Cold Audience)

| Element | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Audience** | Lookalikes, interest-based, broad targeting |
| **Goal** | Introduce the brand and value proposition |
| **Ad types** | Educational content, how-to videos, thought leadership |
| **Budget** | 40% of total ad spend |
| **Key metrics** | CPM, reach, video view rate, landing page views |
| **Success signal** | Low CPM, high video completion, growing retargeting pools |

**Ad examples:**
- Educational: "[Topic] explained in 60 seconds"
- Pain point: "If [frustration], you need to see this"
- Social proof: "[Number] [audience] trust [product]"

### Stage 2: Consideration (Warm Audience)

| Element | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Audience** | Website visitors (7-30 days), video viewers (50%+), social engagers, email list |
| **Goal** | Build trust and handle objections |
| **Ad types** | Case studies, testimonials, demos, comparison content |
| **Budget** | 35% of total ad spend |
| **Key metrics** | CPC, CTR, landing page conversion rate |
| **Success signal** | Decreasing CPC, increasing CTR, page engagement |

**Ad examples:**
- Case study: "How [customer] achieved [result]"
- Demo: "See [product] in action (2-min walkthrough)"
- Comparison: "[Product] vs [alternative]: honest breakdown"
- FAQ: "Your top 3 questions about [product], answered"

### Stage 3: Conversion (Hot Audience)

| Element | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Audience** | Cart abandoners, pricing page visitors, trial users, high-intent page visitors |
| **Goal** | Drive the final conversion action |
| **Ad types** | Direct offer, urgency, guarantee, limited-time discount |
| **Budget** | 25% of total ad spend |
| **Key metrics** | CPA, ROAS, conversion rate |
| **Success signal** | CPA below target, positive ROAS, increasing conversion rate |

**Ad examples:**
- Offer: "[Discount/trial] — limited to [number/time]"
- Urgency: "Your free trial starts now (no credit card)"
- Guarantee: "Try [product] risk-free for [period]"
- Testimonial: "'[Quote about specific result]' — Start yours"

## ROAS Benchmarks by Industry

| Industry | Acceptable | Good | Excellent |
|---|---|---|---|
| E-commerce | 2:1 | 4:1 | 8:1+ |
| SaaS | 3:1 | 5:1 | 10:1+ |
| Lead Generation | 2:1 (by lead value) | 4:1 | 7:1+ |
| Courses / Info Products | 3:1 | 6:1 | 10:1+ |
| Local Services | 2:1 | 3:1 | 5:1+ |

**Interpretation:** ROAS below "Acceptable" means the campaign is unprofitable and needs restructuring. "Good" is healthy scale territory. "Excellent" is where you increase budget aggressively.

## Funnel Health Diagnostics

| Symptom | Likely Cause | Fix |
|---|---|---|
| High Stage 1 CPM, low reach | Audience too narrow | Broaden targeting, test lookalike percentages |
| High Stage 1 spend, tiny Stage 2 pool | Creative not engaging | Test new hooks, video content, pattern interrupts |
| Stage 2 CTR < 1% | Ad-audience mismatch | Refresh creative, test new angles, check frequency |
| Stage 3 CPA > 2x target | Landing page friction or wrong audience | Audit landing page, tighten retargeting windows |
| ROAS declining over time | Creative fatigue | Refresh creative every 2-4 weeks, rotate angles |
| High frequency (>5) on any stage | Audience saturation | Expand audience, reduce budget on that stage |

## Testing Framework

**Priority order (test one at a time):**
1. Audience (who you target — biggest impact)
2. Offer (what you offer: free trial vs demo vs discount)
3. Creative concept (the big idea and visual approach)
4. Headline (specific wording of the hook)
5. Body copy (supporting text)
6. CTA (button text)

**Rules:**
- Run tests for at least 3-5 days or 1,000 impressions per variant
- Statistical significance threshold: 95%
- Kill underperformers at 2x the CPA target
- Scale winners by 20% budget increments (not 2x overnight)
