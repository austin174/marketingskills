# Email Compliance & Industry Benchmarks

Legal requirements and performance benchmarks for email sequences. Include the compliance section in every email sequence deliverable.

---

## Compliance Requirements

### CAN-SPAM (United States)

| Requirement | Detail |
|---|---|
| Physical address | Mailing address required in every commercial email |
| Unsubscribe link | Clear, conspicuous, must work within 10 business days |
| From identity | "From" name and email must be accurate (no spoofing) |
| Subject line | Must not be deceptive or misleading |
| Commercial identification | Message must be identifiable as an ad if applicable |
| Honor opt-outs | Cannot send to unsubscribed contacts; no re-subscribing without explicit consent |

**Penalties:** Up to $50,120 per violation (2024 adjusted). Both sender and the company whose product is promoted can be held liable.

### GDPR (European Union / EEA)

| Requirement | Detail |
|---|---|
| Explicit consent | Opt-in required; no pre-checked boxes |
| Consent documentation | Record when, how, and what they agreed to |
| Right to erasure | Must delete all data on request ("right to be forgotten") |
| Data processing agreement | Required with your ESP (email service provider) |
| Privacy policy link | Must be accessible from signup form |
| Legitimate interest | Can email existing customers about similar products without consent (narrow exception) |

**Penalties:** Up to 4% of annual global revenue or EUR 20 million (whichever is higher).

### CASL (Canada)

| Requirement | Detail |
|---|---|
| Express consent | Required for commercial electronic messages |
| Implied consent | Allowed for existing business relationships (expires 24 months after last transaction) |
| Sender identification | Must identify sender clearly with contact info |
| Unsubscribe mechanism | Required; must process within 10 business days |
| Record keeping | Document all consents with timestamps |

**Penalties:** Up to CAD $10 million per violation (organizations).

### Quick Compliance Checklist

- [ ] Physical mailing address in footer
- [ ] Working unsubscribe link (tested)
- [ ] Accurate "From" name and email
- [ ] Consent documented for every contact
- [ ] Privacy policy linked from signup
- [ ] Data processing agreement with ESP
- [ ] Opt-out honored within 10 days
- [ ] Re-engagement flows respect sunset policy

## Subject Line Formulas

Proven patterns organized by use case:

| Formula | Example | Best For |
|---------|---------|----------|
| Number + Benefit | "3 ways to double your conversion rate" | Educational content |
| Curiosity Gap | "The pricing mistake that cost me $50K" | Story-driven emails |
| Direct Benefit | "Your copy report is ready" | Delivery / welcome |
| Personalization | "[Name], your trial expires tomorrow" | Urgency / onboarding |
| Question | "Are you making this SEO mistake?" | Problem-awareness |
| How-To | "How to write landing pages that convert at 10%" | Educational |
| Social Proof | "Why 5,000 marketers switched this month" | Nurture / launch |
| Urgency | "Last chance: 40% off ends at midnight" | Launch / cart abandonment |
| Pattern Interrupt | "I was wrong about email marketing" | Re-engagement |
| Negative | "Stop wasting money on ads that don't work" | Problem-awareness |

### Subject Line Rules

- Keep under 50 characters for mobile (40 ideal)
- Front-load the most important words
- Use numbers when possible (odd numbers outperform even)
- Avoid spam triggers in excess: "free," "guarantee," "act now," "limited time"
- Personalize with first name in 20-30% of emails (not every one)
- One emoji can increase opens 2-5%, but overuse decreases them
- Preview text (preheader) is as important as the subject — always write both

## Send Timing Benchmarks

### Best Send Times (General)

| Audience | Best Days | Best Times | Avoid |
|----------|-----------|-----------|-------|
| B2B | Tuesday-Thursday | 9-11 AM recipient local | Monday AM, Friday PM |
| B2C | Tuesday-Thursday | 10 AM or 7-9 PM | Weekends (except e-commerce) |
| E-commerce | Thursday-Sunday | 10 AM or 8 PM | Monday-Wednesday for promo |

### Cadence by Sequence Type

| Sequence | Spacing Pattern |
|----------|----------------|
| Welcome | Day 0, 1, 3, 5, 7, 10, 14 |
| Nurture | Every 3-4 days |
| Launch | Announce → Teaser (D2) → Open (D4) → Remind (D6) → Close (D7) |
| Re-engagement | Day 0, 5, 10 |
| Cart Abandonment | 1hr, 24hr, 72hr |
| Cold Outreach | Day 0, 4, 8, 14, 21 |
